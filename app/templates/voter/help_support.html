Welcome to KuraSmart Help & Support 😊

Whether you're casting your first vote, managing an election, or facing a technical issue — we're here to guide you every step of the way. Explore the sections below to make the most of KuraSmart.

👟 Getting Started with KuraSmart

New here? Begin by creating an account or logging in. After logging in, your personal dashboard will appear, giving you access to available elections, notifications, and your profile settings.

  • ✅ Register with your email or student/employee ID.
  • 🔐 Set up two-factor authentication for extra security.
  • 🗂 Browse upcoming and ongoing elections directly from the dashboard.

🗳️ How to Vote Securely

Voting on KuraSmart is designed to be intuitive and secure.

  • 👤 First, ensure you're eligible for the election (your profile will indicate your status).
  • ✅ Select your preferred candidate or option from the list provided.
  • 📩 Click “Submit Vote” and confirm your choice in the pop-up window.
  • 🔒 All votes are end-to-end encrypted and anonymous.

📊 Accessing and Understanding Results

Once voting closes, KuraSmart’s Results page provides clear, transparent data.

  • 📅 Results are available immediately after the election ends.
  • 📈 Visual breakdowns include bar charts, percentage distributions, and voter turnout metrics.
  • 🌍 Filter results by faculty, department, location, or role.

⚠️ Common Issues & Troubleshooting

Having trouble using the platform? Here's how to resolve most issues:

  • 🔄 Try refreshing the page or switching browsers.
  • 📶 Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • 🔐 Log out and log back in if your session has expired.
  • 📨 Still stuck? Reach out via our support form.

👥 Managing an Election (For Organizers)

Organizing an election? KuraSmart provides powerful admin tools:

  • 📝 Create new elections with customizable rules.
  • 📤 Upload candidate lists and voter rolls securely.
  • 🔍 Monitor live participation stats and send reminders.
  • 🛡 Ensure fair practices with built-in audit trails.
