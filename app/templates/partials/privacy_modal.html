KuraSmart Privacy Policy
1. Introduction

KuraSmart is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate our digital voting services effectively. This includes basic profile information (e.g., name, email), voting identifiers, and user activity logs.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for authentication, ensuring vote integrity, providing support, and improving system functionality. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third parties.

4. Data Security

We use strong encryption and multi-layered security protocols to protect your data during transmission and storage. We regularly audit our systems to maintain compliance and prevent breaches.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the intended purposes. Voting records are stored in anonymized form for accountability.

6. Third-Party Services

In some cases, we may use trusted third-party services (e.g., cloud providers) to host or process data. These services are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us via support@kurasmart.com to make a data request or ask questions.

8. Cookies

Our platform uses essential cookies to enhance functionality and secure voting processes. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced clearly, and continued use of the platform implies acceptance of updates.
